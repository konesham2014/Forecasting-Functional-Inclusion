# Impact Model Validation

| indicator      |   actual_2021 |   actual_2024 |   actual_delta_pp |   model_predicted_cumulative_event_effect_pp |   gap_pp |
|:---------------|--------------:|--------------:|------------------:|---------------------------------------------:|---------:|
| ACC_MM_ACCOUNT |           4.7 |          9.45 |              4.75 |                                         8.66 |    -3.91 |

**Interpretation:** The event-driven model explains a meaningful share of the 2021-2024 mobile money ownership growth (Telebirr 2021 launch + M-Pesa 2023 entry), but does not fully close the gap to the observed +4.75pp change. This is consistent with the EDA finding that a large share of *registered* mobile money growth (Telebirr alone: ~27M in 2022 to ~54M in 2026) reflects agent-assisted or dormant sign-ups rather than active, survey-countable usage, and with background secular growth not attributable to any single cataloged event.
